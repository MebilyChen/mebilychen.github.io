@ Pale Director w/ Grimm as the playwriter AU

@ Intense Bug Love

“I can’t do this,” Wyrm said, dropping a stack of drafts on Grimm‘s desk. “rehearsals will have to wait for next week, because your scripts are full of bugs.”
Grimm leaned back in his chair and smiled at the bugs. “Yes, indeed.” He nodded, “And forgive me, for not introducing another species.”
Lurien immediately looked down at his notes, the page he turned over has covered up his face.
Wyrm fixed his gaze on Grimm’s smiling eyes. “I can’t believe you laughed at that.” Slowly and deadly, he glanced back at Lurien, who keeps laughing silently —— also struggling to regain his composure. “S-Sorry sir-” Lurien chuckled and mindlessly scribbled down some notes, still trying to suppress that wild urge.
“Now, ”Wyrm stared into Grimm‘s very soul, “get your screenwriting done before I’m done with you.”
“Aw, please don’t rush me, we still have one more week!”
“It was I who own these seven days,” the pale director narrowed his eyes as he leaned closer, “you only have seven minutes.” He sat down, “And I’ll be here, ‘a-ssis-ting’.”
