出卖了灵魂的web作业
灭绝：弃坑 沙雕 圣巢全员 趣味网站

等着站子上传的时候闲着没事想找点乐子玩，就搜了搜“Interesting Websites”。

顺便捞一下这个帖子，压榨一下它的空间价值（blink）

来源：Hongkiat-blog

Gnoosic

Gnoosic is your go to spot for new music recommendations. It asks for three of your favourite bands, and based on your interests, spits out an artist that you might like.

You then have the option to “like”, “dislike”, or mark it as something you aren’t familiar with – which further refines the results. This will be enjoyable for music enthusiasts who are looking for a musical adventure.

Patatap

Patatap is an interactive website that responds to the keys on your keyboard with a sound and a brief animation.

Now imagine hammering in entire sentences – and you got an explosion of sounds, colors, and movement! Once you start typing in random paragraphs, it becomes almost hypnotic, in a way.

A fun way to unwind and relax.

PostSecret

PostSecret is a very interesting website. Visitors are encouraged to send in anonymous postcards on which they write their secrets.

There are all sorts of secrets on all kinds of postcards, and the variations make this a really interesting project. However, be warned – these secrets are very real… and very heavy.

River Styx

An interactive website that takes you through the river Styx and the Underworld. You will meet many Greek Gods and Goddesses here, and you will also be learning a lot about their myths and legends.

The website works much like how a point-and-click game would, with your cursor turning into a different icon when you hover it over relevant objects. An interesting, visual crash course to Greek mythology and philosophy!

NOIYS – Post, read, forget

NOIYS is a place to post an anonymous note to be viewed by many people, only to be deleted within 24 hours. It’s the perfect website for venting anonymously and not worry about the consequences, as it will be deleted within a day.

The best part (or maybe worst) is that strangers can reply to your note, too. That way, you can have a running conversation with a complete stranger.

MapCrunch

Go on an adventure without leaving your home — because you can’t! This site plops you down in a random location on the globe, and all that’s left to do is explore.

Paper Toilet

Just because stores are sold out of toilet paper doesn’t mean you have to live without. This site features some interactive TP that you can roll up or down.

News of Future

This site was made in 2012 to predict what headlines might look like in the future. The idea was fun enough back then, but now that we’re actually in the future, it’s way more interesting.

Scream Into the Void

Take your outrage about our current situation (or any problem in your life) and throw it into the void. Just type out your feels and then click the “Scream” button, which does exactly what you think it does.

The Wayback Machine

Check out how your favorite websites looked before they got all sleek. Type in a URL, choose a date, and the Wayback Machine will show you a screenshot of the site from that particular day in history.

Star Atlas

If you live in a place with light pollution, it’s probably been a sec since you’ve seen a constellation. Thankfully, Star Atlas has come through with a digital view of the night sky at your location.

2048

This website lets you play a game called 2048, which is kinda like Tetris but with addition. Use your arrow keys to try to combine numbers until you reach 2048, or go beyond and try to reach 4096.

Zoom Quilt

If you’re looking to be hypnotized, then check out this site, which is basically a picture that infinitely zooms in to reveal new pictures.

Freerice

For every correct answer you get on this vocab quiz, “sponsors donate the cash equivalent of 10 grains of rice” to the United Nations World Food Programme. It’s charitable procrastination.

MuscleWiki

MuscleWiki provides an interactive diagram of all the muscles you aren’t moving now that you’re confined to your apartment. Just click on a muscle, and the site will suggest ways to exercise it.

Radiooooo

Radio Garden walked so Radiooooo could run. This site adds a timeline function so you can listen to radio from not just anywhere, but anywhen. Get down to those 1910s Russian bops!

The Passive-Aggressive Password Machine

Type a password (real or fake) into this site and it’ll shade you for how much it sucks.

29a.ch

This interactive site lets you color what looks to be a map of the cosmos, but I’m honestly not sure. Whatever it is, it’s mesmerizing.

My Fridge Food

At this point, your fridge probs has, like, three random items in it, and you’re starting to panic about meal options. Enter My Fridge Food, which inputs everything you have in your kitchen and outputs a recipe. Bless.

Vsauce

Vsauce is one of the few websites on the internet which makes learning fun and interesting. Whenever you have a little downtime and looking for ways to pass it, just head over to the website to learn interesting things and concepts such as the infinite money paradox, the game you win by losing, the napkin ring problem, and more. And if none of those things made any sense, you should definitely visit the website.

A Good Movie To Watch

We all know how difficult finding a good movie to watch can be. It happens with me all the time and I always end up watching the same few movies over and over again. While you can look for the best Netflix movies online, this website is a boon to people like me. They have a highly curated list of movies which you can select based on genre, mood, or just randomly. The best part about this website is that they highlight movies which for one reason or another could not appease the mass media, but are an excellent watch. I have discovered a lot more movies here than anywhere else.

The Useless Web

Want to see what the Internet truly has to offer? Take a peek at The Useless Web to see what truly is out there.

Shady URL

Want to play a harmless prank on your friends or give your co-workers a bit of a scare? If you shorten a URL on this website, it will make it look like the URL will lead to a virus or scam site!

Music Theory

Learn the language behind your favorite pieces of music with this fun website! They offer free lessons and exercises so you have all you need to become the next musical genius.

What Should I Read Next?

Read all the books in your library? This website will take your preferences and give you all the book recommendations you could ever want.

OnRead

If you need a quick and easy book to read, check out OnRead’s library of free ebooks!

Eyebleach

Did you see something on the internet that was just too scary? Click on Eyebleach to be fed adorable pictures of puppies, kitties, or babies!

Weave Silk

Create beautiful mesmerizing works of art on Weave Silk. In addition to being a website, you can also download this onto your phone or tablet.

You Are Listening To

Love true crime and ambient music? You Are Listening To combines police scanner audio and mixes with lofi and other background music to create a one of a kind experience.

Da Font

Tired of your basic Times New Roman? You can spend hours downloading new fonts from typography artists to spice up your new document!

Quick, Draw

Put your drawing skills to the test with Quick, Draw from Google! You’ll help a robot put their doodling skills to the test while also helping Google with machine learning research.

Taste Dive

Looking for new music to spice up your playlists?

HallowUni_Wyrm

白王沃姆是我的空洞宇宙里最最重要的虫子，他虽然不是一切故事的起源，但绝对是这个舞台的主角。
沃姆本来是一个神裔种族，在Cult页面中的Northerwir里会进行详细介绍。此处的沃姆专指白王。
白王的故事可以追溯到他刚出生的时刻。作为一窝小沃姆中最后一只破壳的沃姆，它的父母亲原本不对他的存活抱有任何希望。在其他小沃姆孵出来之后，他的父亲曾经想直接将他的卵连带着他作为其他小沃姆的食物。他的母亲出面制止了他的父亲，就在他们争执不下的时候，他出生了。
他成长得十分艰难，他的父亲从来没有给过他好脸色看，他的兄弟姐妹们也对他百般刁难。但是由于沃姆还是母系社会，只要他的母亲呵护着他，其他家族成员也不好说什么。幼时缺少父爱对他造成了很大的心理创伤，导致他总是缺少安全感和信任感，不善言辞，社交恐惧。这也影响到了他之后的治国方略——消灭一切潜在威胁。
只有在母亲的绒毛里，他才能安稳地入睡，这也是他的专属位置，否则，他就会半夜惊醒。在那个决定性的夜晚，他刚刚经历第三次蜕皮，他做了一个噩梦。梦中的他被一道刺眼的金黄色光芒包围着，数道铁链穿刺进他刚完成蜕皮，还未完全变硬的外壳中，让他动弹不得。他咆哮着，想挣脱束缚，但金黄色光芒却越发明亮，直到他整个视野都充满了光。就在痛苦之时，他听到了一个声音，古老沧桑，他向他诉说了一个悲惨的故事，一个被强行划为奴隶的种族。这个声音和他父亲的声音完全不同，虽然同样威严，但多了一份慈爱。他不懂那些奴隶所受的苦难，但他不可抗拒的被这个声音吸引了。下意识地，他想多听一些他的声音，以此来填补他缺失的父爱。他在母亲的怀中醒来，睁大眼睛回忆着梦中的声音，但发现它已经变得模糊。他幼小的头脑无法完全理解发生在自己身上的事情。那个晚上，所有的沃姆都做了同样的噩梦。
作为神裔种族，他们和梦境界并无瓜葛，也不常去拜访，他们地位位于凡人和半神之间，不像凡人那样梦境是常态，也不像真神那样不会有做梦的欲望。如果不是被迫拽入梦境里或者是自身的欲望过于强烈，他们通常一夜无梦。像这样同时做梦足以引起他们的警惕。沃姆们意识到了，他们运用法术构筑起了围墙，虽然不懂梦境界的法则，但他们有祖辈沿袭的对抗梦境力量的法术。
不过对于这只小沃姆来说，一切已经太晚了。他的噩梦没有因此消失。他想要再一次听到那个声音。
第二夜，他发现自己变小了，变成了渺小的甲虫，他原本的食物之一，而且长出了四肢。他的爪子和腿都被铁链拴着，每走一步都发出叮当的响声。他的背上十分沉重，压得他喘不过气来，他感觉自己在燃烧，腿开始发软。他的前面和后面都是和他差异无几的甲虫，和他一样深陷水深火热之中。有几只雄性蛾子站在一边，爪子里拿着带刺的鞭子，他们看着自己的眼神就像是看着没有生命的工具。
他第一次感受到了愤恨、羞耻，还有嫉妬。他意识到了自己一直是在怎样被对待着，他是怎样无知地忍受着不公的待遇。但他没有说，他装作若无其事的模样，害怕自己的父亲会因为自己的“异变”而杀死自己。
第三个晚上，他看见了“太阳”，那是一只体型巨大的金黄色蛾子，头上的三根尖角构成了她的皇冠。她的面容模糊不清，但他能感觉到自己在被她注视着，嘲笑着。
