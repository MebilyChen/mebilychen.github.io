(（）)

“我迷茫的时候，就会从这里望向泪城。”
“Whenever I feel lost, I will watch the City from afar.”
国王的爪子抚上落地窗，倒影蒙上一层薄薄的蓝。
The King's claw caressed the glass, his reflection shaded with a tint of blue.
“她总能拨开迷雾，让我看清自己前方的道路。”
“Dispersing the mist, she always shows me the direction I should go.”
“看，”他轻声说，“她多美啊。”
“Look at her,” he would whisper, “isn’t she beautiful?”
卢瑞恩默默地站在白王身边，看着雨滴沿着窗檐雕刻的花纹滚落，看着各色的房屋和尖塔隐匿在雨雾之中，看着街道上忽明忽暗的光点和零零星星的过客。但只消片刻，他的视线又回到了白王身上。
Lurien just stood by his side, silently. He stared, as the droplets running down the delicate carvings on the window frame, as various towers and buildings hiding among the rain, as the light flickering and a few passers-by skittering across the street. But it was only after a mere second, that his eyes were back on his King.
稍顷，国王才觉察到对方的注视。他垂下眼，露出略显局促不安的微笑，“——而我…圣巢相信你的存在会让这里变得更加美丽。”他转过身，向他伸出爪子，“所以，你能留在这里吗？”
After a while, the King noticed. He averted his gaze to the ground, smiling, somewhat sheepishly,“—And I…we think your presence here will make her even more beautiful.”He turned around, and extended his claw, “So, are you willing to stay?”

三个点：
1、“你在桥上看风景，看风景的人在看你”
2、“你的存在…更加美丽”双关，一是指将泪城治理得更好，二是指卢瑞恩成为了陛下想来泪城看风景的另一个原因
