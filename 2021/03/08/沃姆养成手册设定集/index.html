Henry Sellars，30
棕发褐眼，粗眉毛，络腮胡
有些迟钝、邋遢
从英国移居到美国的白领
从事文书的工作
大叔脸，少年心
喜欢电音、游戏
标准宅男，暗恋着街心花店的女店主Ana Florence。

（幕后：Henry源于BATIM；Sellars源于很久以前在freem！上面玩的一个文字游戏《阁楼里的暮光》；Ana源于MOTHER中主角之一Ana）

受CS启发！
沃姆有着冬的特质：
沉默、终结、将逝未逝
沉默寡言（智慧）
令人无法抗拒的美丽（损坏艺术品的愧疚）
冰冷（使温度骤降）冰冻的气场 寒凉的气场（特别的寒意徘徊在房间内）
掌握着死亡/将死/复生
无同情心/客观/冷漠

和灯的属性：
过剩的引渡欲望（无论你是否愿意被光照耀，他都会点亮你的世界）
光芒

而陛下转世之后，因为是一个凡尘之神（神降格于凡人之躯） ，所以拥有了感觉和情感。
躯体是可以毁坏的，但神明的精神会继续存在，寻找宿体

冬属性转移：令人无法抗拒的美丽（损坏艺术品的愧疚）

自加属性：
洁癖🌚
反感他人的肢体接触
喜爱寒冷

或者

沃姆制造的宿主容器只是一个玩偶，本身不需要生存能力，所以他不会流血，不会死去，只有当神明元神被击破后才会消失（AO3大佬的设定？不会流血）

His wings flared a dangerously bright light, and it’s the only warning our human could get.
A extreme coldness rushed through the room, like winter suddenly dwelled in. The window literally froze and began to crack, so did other poor thing in this room.
“Oh fuck -”someone hissed from the freezing temperature. Frost began to spread on their skin. The youngest of them trembled and fell, his skin unnaturally pale.
“K-Kim.Kim.Hey. That’s enough.”
“Threats must be eliminated.”He sentenced their death with cold indifference.
“Don’t.You will get yourself in more trouble if you killed someone.”
“Same if I do not.”
“Kim, please-they won’t dare to threat you after this. There’s no need for unnecessary death!”
“I observed,”
“Kim-”
“I know your so-called humanity quite well.”
Another one fell. Unmoving.
“Kim! Listen to me!”
“It is a shame this was taking so long. The process would be much faster if I obtained enough Soul.”
“Just think about this! It’s damn mid-summer! Who will be froze to death in mid-summer?! Right under the bright sun?! The scientists will definitely notice this, and the FBI, and-”
“Let them be.”
“And the whole world will notice you!”
“I am ready for the contact.”
“No you are not. Your wings haven’t healed yet!”
“Stop shouting.You are getting too loud.”
“Not until you realize what a dangerous place you put yourself in! Your power is godlike-”
“I am a God.”
“They will try everything to neutralize you!”
“They will not succeed.”
“They will try EVERYTHING!”
“With no avail.”
“You are getting too self-confident and losing your logical thinking here, Kim!”
“Stop using that mortal name to regard me.”
“Holy Jesus Christ Kim!”
He turned to look at me, only for a short moment, “That was a long title.”
But I don’t feel a sense of humor at all.
“They are dying!”
“I was getting tired of noticing this.”
“Kim-”
“One more word and you will join them as well.”
Fuck.
“I won’t help you to clean up this time.”
“I never asked your help.”
“You better not.”
But still, this is my house.Which means if any dead bodies are found, the first suspect will be me.
Who will believe this thumb-sized bug got a penta-kill?

Kim doesn’t like being touched.
Nor touching anyone else.
Mostly, he will use that little soul magic of his, to get something he wants.
Like, just a wave of hand, a book will float towards him.
He could be dangerous sometimes, as he said, he is a God.
“My! God! Why is there a stuffed teddy bear walking around my study?! ”
“Assisting me the tidings.”
“And what are those ANTS for?!”
“Also assisting.”
“YOU CANNOT OVERUSE YOUR MAGIC!”
“Quiet.”
“UNLESS YOU TURN THEM BACK TO NORMAL!”
He snorted, and another wave of hand, came things breaking sound and ants scattering everywhere.
“God Damn It!”

A giant, terrifying worm, twisted inside the tube.
I can’t tell if it’s looking at me or not. Because said creature didn’t have eyelids, just several black dots surrounded its head.
It’s carapace was white. Pure white. And suddenly I recalled Kim’s words.
I had been enormous before.
“Wyrm.”I gasped.
“Disgusting worm indeed.”Laura replied, “Hurry up Henry, no time to admire this.”
I just stared. What did those bastard do to him?

I felt uneasy.

“Who?”
“The King.”
“You are not supposed to mean-“
“We need to get him out.”

“Are you insane?! This giant monster will get us all killed!”

“Look at this-thing! You said your ‘King’ is a white little bug! ”
