但其实格林晓得他的目的，他最后放任他走了，原因有三：一，他已经从他那里获取了足够的子嗣，二，他比较喜欢征服，而不是养宠物，三，他对白王能如何恢复圣巢很感兴趣，四，bonus，辐光已经挂了所以他现在最终目的——夺回梦境界——已经达到了所以嗯

“…You knew what would happen,” The King gasped, stared the troupe master with rage.”You Knew Exactly What Would Happen To Me When I Reached The Abyss, And You Had Said, That That Is The Only Way To Get Rid Of Her.”
“Well, whether it’s truth or not, I just provided a suggestion, it was you who had chosen your path to—“
“YOU TRICKED ME!”He growled, and began to jerk, trying to loosen the other’s grip.
“Whoa, maybe our pretty butterfly here need to calm down a little bit——”
“LET GO OF Mm——!”
Grimm only pressed him harder, forced The King to bite his lip for preventing a pathetic whimper escape from him.He felt his limb started to grow numb.
“My dear, why are you still so reluctant to—um, bring those adorable little things to this world?”He chuckled, “I thought we’d gotten through this, since we already have a couple of children now.”
“YOU TRICKED ME!”
Grimm seemed puzzled, “You had said that.”
“I LOST MY KINGDOM, I LOST MY FAMILY, I LOST EVERYTHING! IT WAS ALL BECAUSE OF ——“
“Hold on there, “Grimm shifted his position and leaned over his pray.”I adore you, but it doesn’t mean that I would allow you to accuse me of something I never did...besides, my method worked at last, didn’t it?”
“Yes, it worked.Long After My Kingdom Fell And Everything Turned Into Ash!”Suddenly he was getting tired, both physically and mentally.
Have sensed his states, Grimm’s claw slowly climbed to his shoulder, and even with his robe on, The King shuddered at such a heat touch.
”Well, Good things take time, I guess?”Grimm whispered to his ear.
“...stop.”
Came the very soft voice of Wyrm , weak, and quivering.
Grimm tilted his head, with both confusion and amusement.
“...just, make it...stop.Seal me, kill me, abandon me to the abyss, do whatever you need. Hallownest is dead, and so my existence turned meaningless. I’m exhausted, exhausted and unwanted. I want to rest.In Peace.”
“Aw, don’t say that. I want you, sweetheart. Here, I’ll give you a purpose to live. I need you to brood more offsprings for me.”

“...disgusting.”
“Is that so?”He laughed,”while I thought we are doing gods’work, you know? We are creating lives! So divine and admirable.”
Now his claw has slipped under his robe, touching his body. The King felt an urge to vomit.
“And even though you don’t agree with me, my dear King, this is your fate, and you have no choice but to accept it. No turning back, no other way out.”
